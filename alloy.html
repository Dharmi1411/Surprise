The Alloy

It wasn’t just silver — it was every quiet promise melted into form. The kind that doesn’t shout, but gleams gently even when the light fades.

Two rings, one silence — yet they said more than we ever could. The curve of metal catching the glow felt like your laughter turning into forever.

Maybe that’s what love is — an alloy of patience, mistakes, forgiveness, and all the tiny sparks that never stop shining.
